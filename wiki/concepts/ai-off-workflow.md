# AI-Off Workflow

## Definition
AI-Off Workflow is a practice where a user uses AI to gather and organize ideas, then deliberately turns the tool off and rewrites the final output in their own words.

## Why it matters
- It restores ownership of reasoning.
- It strengthens memory and metacognitive connection to the material.
- It reduces the risk of submitting polished text that the user cannot actually explain later.

## Working sequence
1. Ask AI for structure, examples, or candidate explanations.
2. Cross-check key claims across tools or sources.
3. Turn AI off.
4. Rebuild the final answer in personal language from understanding, not transcription.
