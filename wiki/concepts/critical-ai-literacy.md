# Critical AI Literacy

## Definition
Critical AI Literacy is the ability to use AI while continuously questioning its source basis, bias, reliability, and suitability for the current user or task.

## Why it matters
- It prevents users from treating AI output as automatic truth.
- It shifts attention from answer consumption to source inspection and judgment.
- It is a practical survival skill in an environment where AI can sound confident while being wrong.

## Core behaviors
- Ask what data and assumptions likely shaped the answer.
- Compare outputs across multiple tools or viewpoints.
- Check whether the answer fits the user's actual context.
- Preserve final judgment in human language rather than pure copy-paste.
