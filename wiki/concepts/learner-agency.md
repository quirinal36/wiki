# Learner Agency

## Definition
Learner Agency is the ability of a student to actively shape goals, choose resources, make decisions, and take responsibility for how learning happens.

## Why it matters
- It separates passive school participation from real skill growth.
- It allows students to benefit from a dramatically expanded learning environment.
- It functions as a portable advantage even when institutions or tools change.

## Signals of strong agency
- A learner goes beyond assigned material when needed.
- A learner combines school resources with outside tools, courses, or communities.
- A learner uses AI to accelerate understanding, not to outsource responsibility.
- A learner can explain why a learning path was chosen.
