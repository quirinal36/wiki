## Overview
Strategy for integrating `hermes-empire` (Claw-Empire) as the control surface over the [[hermes-agent]] execution layer. Maps high-level CEO directives and project management to granular agent tasks and runs.

## Conceptual Mapping
- **Claw-Empire**: High-level intent, directive, and team orchestration.
- **Hermes**: The runtime/execution layer performing the work.
