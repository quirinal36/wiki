# Seth Godin

Seth Godin is an author and entrepreneur known for his focus on marketing, leadership, and the concept of 'Linchpins' and 'Tribes'.

## Concepts Identified
- **Linchpin**: An indispensable member of an organization who adds human value beyond the manual.
- **Tribes**: Small, connected groups of people shared by a leader and a vision.
