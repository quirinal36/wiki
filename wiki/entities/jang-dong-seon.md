# Jang Dong-seon

## Overview
Jang Dong-seon is presented in this source as a neuroscientist arguing that AI-era education must protect human thinking rather than outsource it blindly.

## Why he matters in this wiki
- He frames AI use as a cognitive and educational problem, not just a tool-adoption problem.
- He emphasizes that trust in AI without questioning sources can distort judgment.
- His position reinforces the need for [[critical-ai-literacy]] and structured workflows like [[ai-off-workflow]].

## Key points from the source
- AI answers reflect accumulated human data and inherit its bias.
- Passive dependence on AI can weaken metacognitive ability.
- Education should reward problem-solving and verification over memorized correct answers.
