# 렛츠코딩앤플레이

## Overview
렛츠코딩앤플레이 is the academy referenced in the source article. In this wiki, it represents a coding-education business shifting from syntax-first instruction toward [[ai-service-building-curriculum]] and student-owned web projects.

## Why it matters
- It is the concrete institution behind the article's argument about education reform.
- It shows how a local academy can respond to AI by emphasizing product thinking, not just exam preparation.
- It connects the abstract idea of [[school-as-learning-hub]] to a real operating context.

## Current positioning
- Reduce dependence on certification-only outcomes.
- Teach students to use AI as a builder's tool.
- Move toward startup-style project execution and communication.
