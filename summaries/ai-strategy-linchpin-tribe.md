# Strategy for Winning in the AI Era: Linchpins and Tribes

This note summarizes a strategic outlook on surviving and thriving in the AI era based on Seth Godin's concepts of 'Linchpins' and 'Tribes'.

## Key Takeaways
- **Linchpins**: Moving beyond manual labor/efficiency to 'Art'—work that cannot be automated or manualized.
- **Tribes**: Shifting from mass reach to 1,000 true fans/connected groups based on emotional resonance and shared vision.
- **Human Value**: Emphasizes emotional connection, leadership through vulnerability, and "The Egg Analogy" (small, meaningful gestures of care).
