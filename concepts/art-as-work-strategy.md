# Art as Work Strategy

In the context of the AI era, 'Art' is defined as the human element of work that cannot be reduced to a manual or handled by an algorithm.

## Distinction from Manual Work
- **AI**: Excellent at following manuals and efficiency.
- **Art**: Focuses on connecting complex interests, starting new movements without permission, and providing emotional resonance.

## Implementation (5 Actions of a Leader)
1. Manifesto
2. Communication with followers
3. Horizontal connection between followers
4. Value-first principles
5. Transparent recording
